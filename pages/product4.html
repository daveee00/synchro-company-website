nRF52840 Bluetooth LE SoC

The product uses low power Bluetooth 5.0 System on Chip that manages wireless pairing, presence broadcasts, and encrypted communication with gateways and mobile devices while maximizing battery life.

Capacitive touch sensor

The product uses a single zone capacitive sensor on the face for one tap authentication and short gestures; designed for reliable detection through light gloves and in varied environmental conditions.

Multi color status LED

The product uses a compact RGB LED that indicates authentication state, battery level, and alerts with programmable color codes and brightness for quick, language independent feedback.

with wireless charging.

The product uses a compact rechargeable lithium battery with integrated wireless charging and power management. An ultra-low-power regulator and optimized sleep modes ensure efficient energy usage and extended operation, while smart charging circuitry provides safe recharging and long battery lifespan.

32GB SSD + RTC

The product uses a small non volatile storage for device identity, short event buffering, and firmware; integrated low power real time clock preserves timestamps during temporary disconnection.
