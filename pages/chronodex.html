TECH SPECS
ESP32-S3

A high-performance microcontroller designed for interactive and connected applications: it integrates Wi-Fi and Bluetooth Low Energy connectivity, enabling stable and secure communication with external systems.
It supports real-time processing, advanced input/output management, and precise control of electronic components.

LED RGB WS2812B ECO

The product uses WS2812B ECO RGB LEDs in SMD 5050 format, each equipped with an integrated driver that allows independent control of color and brightness via a single digital signal. The LEDs operate at 5 V DC, support 16.7 million colors, and can draw up to 60 mA each at maximum white brightness.

4-pin tactile button

The product uses a central 4-pin momentary tactile push button with standard dimensions of 12 × 12 × 7.3 mm, featuring a high-precision mechanism for reliable operation. The button is panel-mounted with secure mechanical fastening and is covered by a 4 × 4 cm round PLA cap, ensuring ergonomic operation and precise tactile feedback.

Stainless Steel Push Button

The product uses a momentary stainless steel push button with a 12 mm round body, designed for panel mounting and robust, reliable mechanical operation. The stainless steel construction provides protection against dust and water, ensuring long-term reliability and durability. This button is an integral part of the product, offering direct and intuitive control to the user and enabling safe and dependable interaction.

Rotary encoder

The product uses a rotary encoder consisting of a compact incremental unit that provides digital rotational input. The encoder generates quadrature signals (A and B) during knob rotation, allowing detection of rotation direction and amount in embedded control systems. The encoder is mounted with a shaft and a PLA knob, making it ergonomic and comfortable to use on control panels.

SSD1306 OLED Display

The product uses a 2.42-inch OLED display module with a 128 × 64 pixel resolution, offering a compact and easy-to-read panel. Its compact form factor and white OLED panel make it suitable for displaying text, graphics, and status information even in limited spaces.
