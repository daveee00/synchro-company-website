WorkFlow is a standalone, pillar shaped, check in terminal engineered for industrial and blue collar environments. It records employee check ins and check outs in real time, tracks working hours, attendance patterns, and daily productivity indicators, and displays clear confirmation messages so workers can verify each registration instantly.
compact vertical presence

The device is tall and slender to maximize visibility while minimizing floor footprint. The stable pillar profile reads easily from a distance without obstructing walkways, while the vertical layout places the display and reader at ergonomic heights for standing users and supports modular mounting options.

TECH SPECS
ESP32-S3

A dual core microcontroller with hardware cryptography and USB OTG support; handles secure TLS communication, peripheral control, and local attendance processing.

WS2812B LED

The product uses an addressable RGB LEDs arranged in a circular ring around the reader; provide programmable color feedback for instant, language independent confirmation and status signaling.

42" IPS display

The product features a high-contrast 5K IPS display with LED backlight and optional capacitive touch. Thanks to its wide viewing angles, accurate color reproduction, and consistent brightness, it ensures excellent readability in professional environments. It clearly displays time, confirmation messages, and simple shift and attendance graphics, enabling fast and reliable visual verification even under varying lighting conditions.

PN532 NFC / RFID module

The product uses a 13.56 MHz contactless reader compatible with ISO14443 and ISO15693 cards and tags; optimized for low latency reads in high throughput environments.

Capacitive fingerprint sensor

The product uses rugged biometric module with onboard template handling and anti spoof features; offers secure, hygienic authentication where required.

32 GB industrial eMMC & power module

The product uses an industrial eMMC for local log buffering and OTA firmware images; integrated power management supports 12–24 V DC input and PoE++ compatibility with surge protection.
