about synchro

Founded in September 2024 under the auspices of the United Nations, SYNCHRO was established to address one of the most pressing challenges of our era: ensuring global coordination through precision, reliability, and innovation.

We are a B2B technology company that brings together engineers, scientists, and designers from all around the world.


Conceived as a technological institution with a civic mission, Synchro brings together engineers, scientists, and policymakers from across the world to design systems that strengthen international collaboration and safeguard the future.
our vision

We aim at becoming a driving force for change toward a better and unified world, where time evolves into a universal resource serving the collective progress.

our mission

We work to provide tools and methodologies to ensure meticulous time measurement and management, enabling the optimization of every aspect of daily life.
