about synchro

Founded in September 2024, SYNCHRO is a B2B technology company that operates to provide tools and methods that ensure, through precision, reliability, and innovation, a meticulous measurement of time to optimise every aspect of daily life.

Conceived as a technological institution with a civic mission, SYNCHRO brings together engineers, scientists, and designers from around the world to design systems that strengthen international collaboration and safeguard the future. Our ultimate purpose is to be the driving force toward a fairer world, in which time belongs to those who deserve it.
