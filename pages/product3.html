DeskFlow is a compact, desk friendly device that organizes an employee’s day into an adaptive, hour by hour plan. It displays a live schedule, nudges focus with gentle reminders, and dynamically reshuffles tasks and breaks when priorities change, helping users reduce decision fatigue and sustain productive momentum.
Desk-friendly size

Designed to sit comfortably on a desk without crowding workspace. Its low profile and slight angle toward the user guarantee optimal glanceability; the rectangular form balances screen area with a minimal footprint so it integrates naturally among office tools.

Matte shell

Durable and premium to the touch; the matte finish reduces glare and fingerprints while signaling a calm, professional presence on the desk.

Soft edge display

A subtly rounded bezel frames the screen to reduce visual clutter and make the interface feel approachable during quick glances.

Tactile action dial

A small, knurled rotary dial provides one handed control for snooze, skip, or quick rescheduling without opening menus, minimizing interruptions.

Ambient progress ring

A low brightness LED ring around the base shows remaining time for the current task at a glance, offering non intrusive progress feedback and gentle pacing cues.

TECH SPECS
ESP32-S3

A high-performance microcontroller designed for interactive and connected applications: it integrates Wi-Fi and Bluetooth Low Energy connectivity, enabling stable and secure communication with external systems.
It supports real-time processing, advanced input/output management, and precise control of electronic components.

3.9" OLED display

The product integrates a high-contrast OLED display with self-emissive pixels and low blue light emission, optimized for time blocks, progress indicators, and short text rendering. It supports capacitive touch input and features precise luminance control to minimize eye strain during extended daily operation.

Touch-sensitive Click Wheel

This interface enables fast, one handed navigation through tasks and quick duration adjustments; integrated click action provides immediate shortcuts (snooze, complete, reschedule) so users can act without opening menus or breaking focus.

Ambient LED light rings

Low brightness, addressable LEDs arranged around the base and the top of DESKFLOW to show subtle progress, reminders, and status cues; programmable colors and dimming allow non intrusive visual nudges visible across a desk while remaining unobtrusive during deep work.

Proximity and ambient light sensors

Deskflow uses infrared proximity sensor wakes the device only when the user is present to conserve power and reduce distractions; ambient light sensor automatically adjusts display and LED brightness for comfortable visibility in varying office lighting conditions.

8 GB industrial eMMC
+ power module (USB C)

Deskflow uses industrial grade eMMC for local storage of preferences, short term logs, and OTA firmware images with wear leveling; integrated power module accepts USB C 5 V input with efficient regulation, surge protection, and low power standby to preserve the RTC and presence sensing when idle.
